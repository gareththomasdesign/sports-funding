Types of available funding

{% from "govuk/components/warning-text/macro.njk" import govukWarningText %} {{ govukWarningText({ text: "Fraudulently claiming or misusing claimed funding is illegal and can lead to prosecution.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

Depending on the type of organisation, there are different options for available funding.

This will cover the following types:

  • Sports organisations that are registered as charities.
  • Sports organisations that are registered as non-profits.
  • Sports organisations that aren't charities or non-profits, but are registered with a recognised governing body.
  • Individual amateur athletes.
